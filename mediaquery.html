SAMSUNG

Samsung, a South Korean multinational conglomerate, is a global leader in electronics, technology, and business. Founded in 1938, it has diversified into various sectors including electronics, construction, and shipbuilding, becoming the largest South Korean chaebol. Samsung is renowned for its innovative products like the Galaxy smartphones and its significant contributions to the semiconductor and display industries.


IPHONE

The iPhone is a line of smartphones developed and marketed by Apple Inc. that runs on the iOS operating system. It combines the functionality of a mobile phone, iPod, digital camera, and personal computer into a single device with a touchscreen interface. The first iPhone was announced by Steve Jobs in January 2007 and launched later that year, revolutionizing the mobile phone industry.


MOTOROLLA

Motorola, now owned by Lenovo, produces a range of Android smartphones under the "Moto" brand, known for their focus on value and user-friendly experiences. The company, which was once a pioneer in mobile communication with devices like the DynaTAC and MicroTAC, has seen a resurgence in recent years, particularly in markets like Brazil and the US.
