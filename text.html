GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming, a term often used interchangeably with climate change, refers to the long-term increase in Earth's average surface temperature. This warming trend is primarily driven by human activities, such as burning fossil fuels, which release greenhouse gases into the atmosphere, trapping more heat and leading to a gradual rise in Earth's temperature.

NEPTUNE

Neptune, the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun, is a giant, blue-colored planet characterized by its strong winds and a thick atmosphere. It is known for being an "ice giant" due to its composition of icy materials, unlike the gas giants Jupiter and Saturn. Neptune's blue color is due to the absorption of red light by methane in its atmosphere.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the ability of machines, especially computers, to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. It involves creating algorithms and software that can analyze data, recognize patterns, and make predictions, leading to advancements in various fields like healthcare, finance, and transportation
